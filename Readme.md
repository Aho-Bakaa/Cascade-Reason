## 🎯 The Problem

Supply chain disruptions cost the global economy hundreds of billions of dollars annually. A port closure, a supplier bankruptcy, or a cyberattack triggers a **cascading failure**—one delay compounds into another, rippling across continents.

LLMs are great at generic Q&A, but they fail at multi-step causal reasoning under uncertainty. They can't simulate how a 7-day port strike affects inventory levels, production schedules, and ultimately customer satisfaction—let alone explain the reasoning step-by-step.

CascadeReason fixes that.
